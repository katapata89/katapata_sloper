FAQ

Common questions before and after using KATAPATA.

Can I use it on a smartphone?

Yes, it runs in the browser. However, detailed adjustments and PDF checking may be easier on a tablet or desktop computer.

Is the print size accurate?

Print size can be affected by printer settings and PDF viewer settings. Before using the draft, always check the printed measurements.

Is there a free output option?

Yes. A quarter-scale sample PDF is available for checking the content and balance of the draft. It is not intended for full-size use.

What is included in paid output?

Paid output is planned as a set including a full-size PDF and an A4 tiled printable PDF. Tops, skirt, and pants are separate output slots.

Is it based on the Bunka women’s sloper?

KATAPATA focuses on creating women’s slopers based on the Japanese Bunka-style drafting method. Please check and adjust the draft according to your purpose.
