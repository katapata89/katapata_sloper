FAQ

Common questions before and after using KATAPATA.

Can I use it on a smartphone?

Yes, it runs in the browser. However, detailed adjustments and PDF checking may be easier on a tablet or desktop computer.

Is the print size accurate?

Print size can be affected by printer settings and PDF viewer settings. Before using the draft, always check the printed measurements.

Is there a free output option?

Yes. A reduced sample PDF is available for checking the content and balance of the draft. It is not intended for full-size use.

What is included in paid PDF export?

Paid PDF export is ¥1,000 per purchase. One purchase lets you export the finalized data as it was at the time of purchase.

The full-size PDF and the A4 tiled printable PDF are both included. You can also save your purchased KATAPATA data and load it again later for export.

If you change the measurements or adjustments after purchase, it will be treated as different data. To export the modified data, a new purchase is required.

Is it based on the Bunka women’s sloper?

KATAPATA focuses on creating women’s slopers based on the Japanese Bunka-style drafting method. Please check and adjust the draft according to your purpose.
