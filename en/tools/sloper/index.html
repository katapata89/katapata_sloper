Before you start

Measurement units

The app supports both centimeters and inches. Internal drafting calculations are based on centimeters.

PDF output

The free output is a quarter-scale sample PDF. Paid output provides a full-size PDF and an A4 tiled PDF as a set.

Bodice and sleeve slopers

KATAPATA focuses on women’s slopers based on the Japanese Bunka-style drafting method.

Skirt and pants slopers

Skirt and pants slopers are handled as separate output slots.
