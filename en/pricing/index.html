Pricing & PDF Export

You can check your draft for free, then export the finalized data at the time of purchase as printable PDFs when needed.

Free

What you can do for free

You can enter measurements, preview your sloper, make adjustments, and export a reduced-size sample PDF. The sample PDF is for checking the draft and overall shape. It is not full size.

Paid

What you can do after purchase

You can export the finalized data at the time of purchase as both a full-size PDF and an A4 tiled PDF. This is not priced separately by item; one purchase covers the finalized data together.

Purchased Data

About purchased data

After purchase, you can save the finalized data from that moment. When you reload the saved purchased data, you can export the same data again later.

If you change the measurements or adjustments after purchase, it will be treated as different data. To export the modified data, a new purchase is required.

Before printing

When using the pattern draft, always check the printed measurements before cutting fabric. The reduced-size sample PDF cannot be used for full-size measurement checks.
