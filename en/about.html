Introduction

What is KATAPATA?

KATAPATA is a browser-based tool for drafting sewing slopers.
Enter your measurements, preview the draft, make small adjustments, and move on to printable PDFs.

Simple Sloper Making

Make sloper drafting simpler.

KATAPATA organizes the process into measurement input, preview, adjustment, confirmation, and output. You can start by entering your measurements and checking the draft on screen.

What you can do

1

Enter measurements

Enter bust, waist, back length, and other required measurements in centimeters or inches.

2

Preview the sloper

Check the generated draft and make small adjustments if needed.

3

Export PDFs

Use a free quarter-scale sample PDF, or a paid printable PDF set.

Full-size PDF and A4 tiled PDF are provided as a set

Paid output is planned as a printable set that includes both a full-size PDF and an A4 tiled PDF. Output slots are separated for tops, skirt, and pants.
